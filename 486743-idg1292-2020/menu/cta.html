Call to action

To ensure sustainable consumption and production might seem like a lofty goal and unattainable for school children. But the best way to get this goal on the way is if everyone does a little in areas that affect you directly. So here is a CALL TO ACTION for all the teachers and youth organizers out there. #ggc2021

The grand GARBAGE CUP 2021

All grade schools in a county will compete against each other in picking garbage and cleaning up their surrounding area.

Points given out for each hg of garbage but weighted after the materials recyclability.

  • Per 1hg of plastic: 2 points
  • Per 1hg of paper/cardboard: 5 points
  • Per 1hg of metals: 10 points

Three stages of competition.

Every tier consists of a group of 3 students, one student per bag.

Tier 1

  • One class against each other. The best group advances to the next stage.

Tier 2

  • Each class in the school against each other. (Weighed more for the younger students so they can be competitive)

Tier 3

  • All the elementary schools in your area/county against each other.

The grand prize is a full day, where the winning class, gets to see the entire cycle of what the garbage that they picked. From garbage to refined and processed materials and what sort of products recycled raw materials get utilized by local businesses in their area.

Post yourself participating in the THE GRAND GARBAGE CUP 2021 with the hashtag #ggc2021 on social media!
